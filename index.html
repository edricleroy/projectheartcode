What is Histritonic personality disorder?

Histritonic Personality Disorder is a personality disorder characterized by a pattern of attention-seeking behaviors which usually begin in childhood, such as inappropriate seduction and an excessive need for approval.

Did you know that?

Only 1% of the world's population will have this personality disorder. However the percentage has increased through out these few years. From 1% to 2.1% in 2022
